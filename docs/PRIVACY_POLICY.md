# Word a Day — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 28, 2026

This page is the public privacy policy for **Word a Day**, an offline Russian–English dictionary for iOS with optional daily word reminders. A shorter summary also appears inside the app under **Settings → Legal & privacy**.

## 1. Data collection

Word a Day is designed with a privacy-by-default architecture. The developer does **not** collect, store on servers, or receive personal information such as your name, email address, precise location, contacts, or search history. Dictionary lookups run entirely on your device against a bundled database. The app does not require an account.

## 2. Data stored on your device

To work offline, the app keeps some information locally on your iPhone or iPad, for example:

- The bundled dictionary database and search index.
- Your notification schedule and pronunciation preferences.
- Which words have been used for daily reminders, favourites, and recently viewed words.

This information stays on your device unless you delete the app or use in-app controls to reset relevant data (for example, resetting used words). It is not transmitted to the developer.

## 3. Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app schedules **local** alerts on your device at times you choose. Notification text (Russian headword and English gloss) is set when each reminder is scheduled on the device. The developer does not operate a remote push notification service and does not receive notification content, device tokens, or other identifiers from your device for tracking purposes.

Apple’s system may handle delivery of local notifications on your device; that processing is between you, your device, and Apple’s platform services.

## 4. Local processing and audio

Pronunciation uses your device’s built-in text-to-speech (TTS). Audio is generated locally; voice data and spoken text are not sent to the developer or to third-party speech servers operated by the developer.

## 5. Third-party services in the app

The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking. Dictionary content is compiled into the app at build time from licensed open data (see [DATA_LICENSES.md](../DATA_LICENSES.md) in the project repository). The app does not download dictionary entries from the internet during normal use.

## 6. Open data and legal credits

The offline dictionary incorporates material from [OpenRussian.org](https://en.openrussian.org/) (CC BY-SA 4.0), example sentences from [Tatoeba](https://tatoeba.org/) contributors where needed (typically CC BY 2.0 FR), and frequency data from [FrequencyWords](https://github.com/hermitdave/FrequencyWords) (MIT).

## 7. Children

The app is intended for a general audience. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## 8. Changes

This policy may be updated when the app or this page changes. The “Last updated” date above reflects the current published version.
